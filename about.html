WHO I AM?

I am currently an Associate Director, working within the RSM Transaction Services team in London. I have 8+ years due diligence experience, and I have been specialising in data analytics for transactions for the last three years.

I have worked across a broad variety of buy and sell-side deals (in the context of sale, acquisition, fundraising or refinancing processes) for both private equity and corporate clients in the mid market space, helping companies to produce innovative solutions to complex problems and visualise results from new digital technologies.

My background in the accounting, transaction, and analytics areas place me well to understand business problems whilst solving them quickly and efficiently with my analytical skillset. My experience enables me to unlock hidden business insights, presenting these in an interactive manner and allowing the user to explore their company at a deeper level.

My combined financial and data analytics experience over the last three years enabled me to develop a number of business case, including the following three projects detailed in this portfolio website:

  • A bespoke dynamic PowerBI dashboard, securely hosted online, allowing target business and potential buyers to interact with the analysis;
  • The production of a dynamic financial data-book (in Microsoft Excel), which helped potential buyers to analyse the business in an engaging way; and
  • An ARR bridge by client analysis, with key information sourced from the client's ERP, based on a CSV. extract

From a technical point of view, my experience helped me to develop advanced data modelling, manipulation and visualisation skills, with practical experience in machine learning and business intelligence tools (Alteryx, Power BI, Power Query, Power Pivot).
I've also build up coding experience in multiple languages, including for example: DAX, VBA, SQL, or Python. See certifications here
